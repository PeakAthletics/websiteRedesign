Mental Health Support for Physicians

What You Need, Where You Need, When You Need

Physicians are modern day heroes. They see horrendous traumas and work relentlessly to provide care. Day-in and day-out, physicians:

  • see the human body suffer from disease and calamities
  • risk deadly exposures
  • experience the stress of medical errors and complications
  • feel restricted by the insurance and resource limitations that cause moral injury

The impact of traumatic events on physicians is devastating.

24%

of physicians report being clinically depressed

https://www.fiercehealthcare.com/providers/physicians-are-twice-likely-general-population-attempt-suicide-medscape-survey-found

1 in 10

training physicians developed PTSD at the end of their first internship year

https://jamanetwork.com/journals/jamanetworkopen/fullarticle/2780781

1 in 11

physicians reported having suicidal thoughts

https://www.fiercehealthcare.com/providers/physicians-are-twice-likely-general-population-attempt-suicide-medscape-survey-found

Physicians have the highest suicide rate of any profession with a rate twice that of the general population.

https://www.medscape.com/viewarticle/896257

There needs to be accessible and appealable mental health support for physicians. There needs to be an app that provides physicians What You Need, Where You Need, When You Need.

Thus, we built the WYN Health app.
