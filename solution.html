Our Solution

WYN Health App

WYN Health is a mobile app that provides asynchronous therapy and peer support -- a supplementary mental health tool that gives physicians “What You Need, Where You Need, When You Need.”

How to use the app:

During transition times in the hospital, physicians record 5-minute text/audio/video messages that can be sent to therapists or a customized peer group. Peers will respond to messages when they have transition time in their shift schedule, and therapists will have scheduled response times.
The app has a dedicated resources page where physicians can access physician-focused wellness content and find crisis hotline information. The app is not a crisis tool, and if a physician is in crisis, they should reach out to a hotline for an immediate response.

Protecting your privacy is our priority:

The messaging platform is HIPAA compliant, with six encrpytion methods and advanced authentication to securely store user data.

WYN Health's features provide clinically validated support:

Scientific studies report that asynchronous therapy results in symptom reduction and a higher treatment commitment than that of traditional face-to-face therapy. Additionally, studies reveal that offering peer support allows physicians to feel that they are “not alone” and believe that “recovery is possible.” Moreover, study participants who used asynchronous peer support tools report statistically significant improvements in their mental health. Thus, WYN Health is not only an appealable support tool that addresses physicians' user needs but also an effective treatment delivery mechanism.
