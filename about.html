About Us

Amanda Levy, CEO and Founder

Amanda graduated from Brown University with Magna Cum Laude honors in Computer Science - Economics and a Certificate in Entreprenuership. Amanda became interested in public health when she ran a research study and wrote a paper about PTSD and anxiety disorders. As her sister started her cardio-thoracic surgery residency, she learned more about the intensity of physicians' work and began interviewing physicians about the corresponding mental health impact.
Amanda interviewed over 65 physicians about the micro- and macro-trauamas that they experience as a physician. She learned about the barriers to mental health support and what they would find accessible and appealable support. She talked with physicians at numerous hospital systems, such as MGH, Stanford, University of Michigan, Northwestern, University of Utah, Brown, and many others. The physicians ranged across specialities, including EM, General Surgery, OB-GYN, anesthesiology, and more.
Based on these physicians' experiences, she built WYN Health to deliever mental health support that offers physicians What You Need, Where You Need, When You Need.
